HI, I'M MARISSA!

Howdy! I'm Marissa Turnage, a web developer and interface designer with a penchant for illustration and graphic design.

I'm a geek for beautiful websites that move fast and work perfectly. Molded by my life experiences, I have a foot firmly in both the design and development camps. Working mostly with small businesses and startups, I love helping to distinguish them from the crowd through personalized, effective websites.

My artsy side has been present since day one. Spending my childhood drawing and doing crafts, I later found my passion for music through the violin and learned how to work hard, practice towards success, and express myself.

My interests turned out to align perfectly: playing an instrument is an incredibly technical activity, and requires serene, monolithic focus - just like programming. I have an ingrained respect for tradition, high art, and devotion to my craft, as well as an instinct for process, time management, and quality control.
